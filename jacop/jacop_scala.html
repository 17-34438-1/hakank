JaCoP/Scala

JaCoPinScala is a Scala wrapper for JaCoP. Download from Sourceforge

Here are some of my JaCoP/Scala programs.
  • AllDifferentExcept0.scala: Decomposition of alldifferent_except_0
  • AlldifferentTest.scala: Alldifferent test
  • AllIntervals.scala: All intervals (CSPLib #7)
  • BowlsAndOranges.scala: Bowls and oranges problem
  • CircuitTest.scala: Test of a decomposition of circuit as well as an "path extractor" for the circuit.
  • CircuitTest0Based.scala: Test of a decomposition of circuit as well as an "path extractor" for the circuit. This variant use a 0-based arrays.
  • CoinsGrid.scala: Coins grid problem
  • DeBruijn.scala: de Bruijn sequence
  • Diet.scala: Simple diet opimization problem
  • Diet2.scala: Simple diet opimization problem (alternative)
  • DivisibleBy9Through1.scala: Divisible by 9 through 1
  • FurnitureMoving.scala: Furniture moving, a simple scheduling problem (using cumulative)
  • Hidato.scala: Hidato grid puzzle
  • Langford.scala: Langford's number problem
  • LeastDiff.scala: Least difference problem
  • LeastDiff2.scala: Least difference problem (alternative)
  • MagicSquare.scala: Magic square
  • MapColoring.scala: Map coloring
  • Minesweeper.scala: Minesweeper
